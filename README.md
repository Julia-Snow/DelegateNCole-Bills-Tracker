# Delegate N.T. Cole - Bills Tracker

This repository tracks all legislative bills sponsored by Delegate N.T. Cole (Virginia House of Delegates).

## House Bills (HB)

### HB 419 - Approval of land use applications; residential development
- **Chief Patron**: Cole, N.T.
- **Status**: Continued
- **Summary**: Requires every locality to take final action to approve, approve with conditions, or deny any land use application for the rezoning or development of property that includes residential development within 12 months from the date of submission.

### HB 420 - Public utilities; reopening rate case in certain instances
- **Chief Patron**: Cole, N.T.
- **Patrons**: Cole, N.T., Shin
- **Status**: Continued
- **Summary**: Provides that if an application is filed with the State Corporation Commission for approval of the acquisition or disposition of control involving a public utility and such application is filed within 24 months after the entry of a final order in a rate increase proceeding, the Commission may reopen such rate increase proceeding.

### HB 421 - Eminent domain; condemnation of public waterworks systems
- **Chief Patron**: Cole, N.T.
- **Status**: Continued
- **Summary**: Requires the body determining just compensation in a condemnation proceeding initiated by a locality for the taking of a waterworks system to consider the difference between rates, fees, and charges for water service.

### HB 422 - Water utilities; prohibition of multiple rate increases within 3-year period
- **Chief Patron**: Cole, N.T.
- **Patrons**: Cole, N.T., Anthony, Bennett-Parker, Carroll, Clark, Cole J.G., Feggans, Garrett, Nivar, Price, Shin
- **Status**: In Committee
- **Summary**: Prohibits a public utility authorized to furnish water or water and sewer service from filing an application for a rate increase more frequently than once in any three-year period, with exceptions for decreases, adjustments, emergency increases, and extraordinary circumstances.

### HB 1165 - Division superintendents; Board of Education regulations
- **Chief Patron**: Cole, N.T.
- **Patrons**: Cole, N.T., McPike
- **Status**: Passed
- **Summary**: Requires the Board of Education to amend its regulation relating to the qualifications of division superintendents, including requirements for good character, demonstrated ability as an educational administrator, and specific experience requirements.

### HB 1176 - Regulatory boards; quorum requirements
- **Chief Patron**: Cole, N.T.
- **Patrons**: Cole, N.T., Krizek
- **Status**: Pending Governor's Communication
- **Summary**: Lowers quorum requirements for the Board for Architects, Professional Engineers, Land Surveyors, Certified Interior Designers and Landscape Architects, and the Board for Professional Soil Scientists, Wetland Professionals, and Geologists.

### HB 1436 - Virginia Erosion and Stormwater Management Program authority
- **Chief Patron**: Cole, N.T.
- **Status**: Continued
- **Summary**: Removes the requirement for a Virginia Erosion and Stormwater Management Program (VESMP) authority to have a performance bond with surety, cash escrow, or letter of credit to enter property for maintenance actions.
